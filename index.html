About Me

Hi, I'm Amanda Innis and I'm a User Experience (UX) and User Interface Designer.

Included are examples of my work: my client project for "Charlie," a marketing management site; "Syne," an event curation app; as well as other projects I completed in the User Experience Design Immersive program at General Assembly in New York City.

I have a background in art and design which helps me to question and clarify the presentation of information. I also love to create engaging layouts and icons. My empathy, openness to change and belief in the creative process helps me to iterate on my designs based on user needs and business goals.
