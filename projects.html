Meeting Charlie

Who is Charlie?
Charlie is your small business Marketing Manager.

What does Charlie do?
Charlie is your business partner who will help you recruit and manage the freelancers you need to grow your business.

Who Are Charlie's competitors?
Charlie competes with websites like Crew, Traction, and Gigster who help you find freelancers for any project you have in mind.

What is Charlie's brand personality?
The Charlie platform is trustworthy, approachable, and convenient.

What is the Charlie business model?
The Charlie platform is your small one-stop shop to connect your small business to the partners you need to grow.

Who is Charlie's target market?
The Charlie platform targets small business owners, marketing managers, and freelancers.
